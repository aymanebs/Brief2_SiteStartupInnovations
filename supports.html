FAQ

Have questions? Here you'll find the answers most valued by our partners, along with access to step-by-step instructions and support.

  • Our easy-to-use owner dashboard will be the primary location for managing your reservations, updating rates and keeping your listing up to date. Some partners choose to use a software provider to link our systems. They still log in to the owner dashboard to leverage our more advanced tools like MarketMaker and Boost.

  • If you haven't found what you are looking for, visit our Help Center for step-by-step guidance on how to use our products.

  • Integrated property managers use third-party or proprietary software that they have connected to our marketplace to manage their listings. Integrated property managers can manage thier listings directly from their software by an integration feed that automatically updates reservations, amenities, photos and more.

  • Our mission is to bring the world within reach through our partners. Our goal is to provide our partners with the most bookings possible and to do so; we need to provide travelers with a simple and secure online booking experience.
